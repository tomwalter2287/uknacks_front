Together with members of the college community, Uknack is building a more authentic, human and sustainable online marketplace where every student feels encouraged and supported to start their own businesses.

Welcome to the “Uknack Economy”

In the Uknack Economy, creative college entrepreneurs can create fun and fulfilling jobs by selling items and services to local College communities, where thoughtful consumers can discover those items and services and build lasting relationships with their classmates who create and sell them.

A Uknack economy empowers and encourages artists, designers and makers to start and grow their very own businesses.

It’s a social marketplace that connects buyers and sellers around the college community to connect, engage and do good business together.

By building and supporting our local economy, we hope to inspire students to discover their passions, create fulfilling jobs and lead happier and more hopeful lives.

Making Good with Good Business

We believe in the transformative power of good business, Good business can inspire practices that are sustainable, humane, and profitable for everyone involved. Uknack is proud to support excellent college entrepreneurs who wish to use their businesses as a way to make a difference in the world.
