Simple Tools for Simply Doing Business

Easy To Use Search

Uknack’s easy to use search bars let’s you instantly find the right items and services by classmate name, college, category or price.

Hire With One Click

Hire friends and classmates to help you get anything you need done. Once you’ve found someone, simply hire them and they’ll take care of the.

Verified Students

Only students with a .edu email address can join Uknack, so you can rest assured that your classmates identity has been verified.

Upfront Pricing

No more haggling over rates. All prices are upfront and final so you’ll pay only what your classmates quoted and nothing more.

Seemless Payments

Put away your wallet. Once your service is complete, easily pay your classmates with Venmo, Paypal or cash. You’ll also receive a receipt.

Trustworthy Reviews

After your service is complete, you can rate your experience and view ratings about other classmates before you hire them.
